Anna Wren, Morgan Curtis Properties

What We Built

  • Full business realignment including ideal client definition and core offer refinement
  • Complete website overhaul aligned to the new positioning
  • Full CRM and lead pipeline in GoHighLevel
  • Automated flow from social comment to investor pack delivery
  • Notion project management for the whole team
  • Custom AI tools for SOPs and client communications
  • AI chatbot live on the website
  • Email triage system saving hours every week
  • In-person team training in Manchester
  • Ongoing strategy coaching, bi-monthly
GoHighLevel Automation Notion Custom GPTs AI Chatbot Team Training

The Situation

A fast-growing property investment business with a founder doing everything herself. Strong deal flow, a growing team, and no infrastructure to keep pace. Social media leads were disappearing into inboxes. The team had no shared system. Every client interaction was manual.

What We Built

I mapped every process from a social media comment to a completed investor pack, then automated the whole sequence. A potential investor who comments on a post now moves through a fully automated pipeline and receives a professionally designed pack with no manual effort required.

In parallel: a Notion workspace for the full team, custom GPTs for SOPs and client communications, and an AI chatbot handling initial website enquiries.

The Training

I ran in-person training sessions in Manchester with the whole team. Not just showing them how it works but making sure they understood why, so they can iterate and improve it themselves.
